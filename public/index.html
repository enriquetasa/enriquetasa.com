enrique tasa

- software engineer -

I was born in 1997 in Valencia, Spain. I was lucky to speak both Valenciano and Spanish natively.
I learnt English at school and now speak it at a bilingual level.

I moved to the UK and studied Electronic Engineering at the University of Manchester.
I met some great people, had a lot of fun - eventually I graduated with an upper second class honours degree.

I was an active alumni of the European Youth Parliament for 5 years.
For some time, I was the president of EYP España.
EYP taught me public speaking and the importance listening.

I did an industrial placement at Alfatec Sistemas for a year, writing C# code for companies like Mercedes Benz.
There I realised I loved software and decided that's what I wanted to do professionally.

After university I worked at DAI for some time.
I was a member of the core tech team and mostly worked on implementing the infrastructure needed to modernise development practices.

Currently, I work for ihateironing.com, where I'm helping them advance their engineering vision forward.

I love to read. You can find some of my favourite books and recommendations here.
