- la vida puede ser maravillosa -

I was born in 1997 in Valencia, Spain. I was lucky to speak both Valenciano and Spanish natively.
There I still have my family and a good bunch of friends.

I moved to the UK and studied Electronic Engineering at the University of Manchester.
There I perfected my English and learnt a little about engineering. Eventually I graduated with an upper second class honours degree.

I worked at Alfatec Sistemas for a year, writing C# code for companies like Mercedes Benz.
There I realised I loved software and decided that's what I wanted to do professionally.

I was an active alumni of the European Youth Parliament for 5 years.
For some time, I was the president of EYP España.
There I practiced my public speaking and learnt the importance listening.

After university I started working at DAI.
I specialise in Continuous Integration and internal tooling and mostly use Python.

I love to read. You can find some of my favourite books and recommendations here.
